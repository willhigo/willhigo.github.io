Will Higo

Director • Editor

{% include byline.html %}

Will is a London based writer, director and editor with over a decade’s experience in promos, behind the scenes documentaries and short form drama. Will has worked worldwide, crafting content for clients such as Universal, Film4, Netflix, Disney and Sony Pictures.

In 2010, his screenplay ‘Stay Awake’ was optioned and subsequently entered development with Academy Pictures. In 2014, he successfully pitched the feature film ‘The Group’ to Agile Films as part of the Agile Incubate scheme, working with director Max Myers to develop the treatment.
